One Piece Film: Red

 One Piece Film: Red follows Luffy and the Straw Hat Pirates as they attend a long-awaited concert by Uta, the world’s most beloved singer—and Shanks’ daughter. But when shocking secrets about Uta’s powers and her past come to light, the crew is pulled into a battle that could shake the world. Filled with music, action, and emotional twists, this film is a must-watch for every One Piece fan.
